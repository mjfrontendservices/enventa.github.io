About En venta

En venta is taken from Spanish word which means (on sale). En venta was created to provide a place where local sellers or online seller can able to sell thier product without worrying about marketing and selling. Customers can easily spot the product they want to buy by just typing on the search bar. People can search products and even prices.
En venta is a light weight static website. It is running without any server side language to ensure it's security and performance (loads fast) compare to other database driven websites.
